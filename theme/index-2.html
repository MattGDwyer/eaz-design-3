Our Recent Projects

NURSERY

Unfinished attic finished! Just in time! The baby arrived and this is his new cozy spot with his family. Light and bright, insulated and safe. Great nursery ensuite! — in Columbia City.

BATHROOM

This "Theirs" bathroom is a total transformation; custom vanity and tile design throughout, including the bath surround. The clients wanted to keep that original window as part of the design. Good call!

BAY

This bay turned out so well. It will be a cozy gathering place for family meals, or just gazing out the windows. Simple and it fits right in with the house and the neighborhood.

DECK DESIGN

Elevated deck design for a build that had not one but two Environmental Critical Area designations. An ECA can really complicate and slow down any permitting process. However, we were able to get both done through STFI.

PATIO, FENCE

Check out this beautiful outdoor project we did in Judkins Park. The future patio space was unusable and hidden behind a very tall wall of Aborvite. We took a basic layout the clients had and designed a custom patio, fence and garden beds in the right of way. Let the sunshine in!

Bathroom Entry

Really pleased with how this bathroom entry cabinetry built-in turned out. Plenty of storage added. — in Hillman City.
