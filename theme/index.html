Our Values

We are in the business of getting things done right. We pride ourselves on craftsmanship of extraordinary quality. We are also proud members of our local community valuing mutual respect, sustainability for the future, and lasting client relationships.

We don't cut corners. Our bespoke designs are always to code. Every project is fully and properly permitted. Our company, and subcontractors are fully insured.

All of our projects are reflections of us, our clients, and their homes. As such, we bring our creativity to your project, and match the design to your needs, and the style of your home.

Seattle is a diverse place! So is our clientelle. Our clientelle represents the true demographic diversity of our community. We celebrate diversity!
